Project Description

Green Crowd is a shopping utility application that aims to help consumers buy green on a budget. By crowd sourcing the collection of pricing information, Green Crowd can show shoppers exactly how much it will cost to shop at a given store. With this, we hope to reward shoppers that are already buying green and enable shoppers that previously abstained from buying sustainable goods due to prohibitivly high prices.
